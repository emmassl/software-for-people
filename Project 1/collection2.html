Title: The Queenʻs Quilt, or Imprisonment Quilt

Author: Queen Liliʻuokalani

Date Created: 1895

In 1895, while imprisoned at ʻIolani Palace during the illegal overthrow of the Hawaiian Kingdom, Queen Liliʻuokalani, along with two Ladies-in-Waiting, constructed this quilt with the focus of protecting her people and sharing her story. Partially constructed with fabric from the dresses of women who supported her, itʻs a powerful symbol of her memories and the solidarity of her people.
